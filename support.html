How do I copy a prompt to use in ChatGPT?
Tap on any prompt from the list, then tap the "Copy to Clipboard" button. You can then paste it directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any other AI platform.
How do I save prompts to my favorites?
Open any prompt detail view and tap the heart icon in the top-right corner. All your favorite prompts will appear in the "Favorites" tab.
Can I create my own collections of prompts?
Yes! In the prompt detail view, tap "Add to Collection" and either select an existing collection or create a new one. Manage your collections from the "Favorites" tab.
How do I open prompts directly in AI apps?
In the prompt detail view, use the "ChatGPT", "Claude", or "Gemini" buttons to open the respective apps with your prompt pre-filled. If the app isn't installed, it will open in your web browser.
How do I search for specific prompts?
Use the search bar at the top of the Prompts tab. You can search by keywords, categories, or filter by AI recommendations and favorites.
What's in the Learn tab?
The Learn tab contains three sections: ready-to-use templates you can customize, prompting techniques from beginner to advanced, and daily challenges to improve your AI prompting skills.
Will my data be saved if I close the app?
Yes! All your favorites, custom collections, and preferences are automatically saved and will be restored when you reopen the app.
